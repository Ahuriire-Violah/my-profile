Karibu Groceries System

Karibu Groceries is a powerful inventory management system designed to help grocery store owners efficiently manage their shops. It allows smooth tracking of purchases, sales, and stock levels in real time.

The system simplifies store operations by providing quick access to stock reports, purchase orders, and sales transactions, which helps reduce losses and improve customer service.

Key Technologies: Django, Python, MySQL, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
